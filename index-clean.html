What TouchBarFix Does

TouchBarFix restarts Touch Bar processes when they freeze or become unresponsive. Launch the app when your Touch Bar shows a black screen or stops responding to touches.

Terminal Alternative

You can achieve the same result using Terminal:

sudo pkill TouchBarServer

TouchBarFix provides a convenient app interface for this command.

Important: This only fixes software-related freezing. Hardware problems require professional repair.

Requirements

  • MacBook Pro with Touch Bar (2016-2020)
  • macOS 10.14 or later
  • Administrator password
