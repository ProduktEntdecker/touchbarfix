## ‚úÖ CONTENT APPROVAL PROCESS

**Before publishing any content:**

1. **Fact Check**: Does everything match the authentic story?
2. **Voice Check**: Does tone match brand personality guidelines?
3. **Value Check**: Does it provide genuine help beyond product promotion?
4. **Honesty Check**: Are limitations and alternatives clearly mentioned?
5. **Consistency Check**: Does messaging align with website/Gumroad copy?

**Red Flag Review Questions:**
- Would I be embarrassed if this was fact-checked?
- Does this help people regardless of whether they buy?
- Am I being honest about my expertise level?
- Are all claims verifiable and authentic?
