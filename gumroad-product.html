What This Tool Does

TouchBarFix is a simple utility that restarts your MacBook's Touch Bar when it freezes or becomes unresponsive. It's essentially a convenient graphical interface for Terminal commands that restart the Touch Bar process.

How It Works

  1. Download and open TouchBarFix.app
  2. Click "Fix Touch Bar" when your Touch Bar is frozen
  3. Wait a few seconds for the Touch Bar to restart

That's it. No password required, no complex setup.

What It Can Fix

  • Touch Bar showing black screen
  • Touch Bar not responding to touches
  • Touch Bar buttons stuck or frozen
  • Unresponsive ESC key on Touch Bar

Important Limitations

  • Cannot fix hardware problems - Physical damage won't be helped
  • Not a prevention tool - Doesn't stop issues from happening
  • Not a permanent fix - Hardware issues will return

Free Alternative

You can achieve the same result using Terminal:

pkill TouchBarServer
killall ControlStrip

TouchBarFix simply provides a button interface for these commands. If you're comfortable with Terminal, you don't need this tool.
