What TouchBarFix Does

TouchBarFix is a utility app that restarts Touch Bar processes on MacBook Pro computers. It:

  • Restarts Touch Bar software processes when they freeze
  • Requires administrator privileges to function
  • Only works on MacBook Pro models with Touch Bar (2016-2020)
  • Does not modify system files or install background processes

Important Limitations

TouchBarFix only fixes software-related Touch Bar freezing. It cannot repair hardware damage, water damage, or genuine hardware failures.

If TouchBarFix doesn't solve your issue, you likely need professional hardware repair.
