Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy

Simple Truth

TouchBarFix collects no personal data. The app runs locally on your Mac, doesn't connect to the internet, and doesn't track you.

What TouchBarFix Does

TouchBarFix is a simple utility that restarts Touch Bar processes on your Mac. It:

  • Runs entirely on your local Mac
  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your usage
  • Does not store any data about you

Data Collection

We collect no data. TouchBarFix:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not use analytics or tracking
  • Does not send data to servers
  • Does not use cookies or similar technologies
  • Does not access your files or documents

Website Privacy

Our website (touchbarfix.com) uses minimal tracking:

  • Plausible Analytics: Privacy-focused website analytics (no personal data)
  • No cookies: We don't use tracking cookies
  • No user accounts: We don't store user profiles

Support Contact

When you contact us for support:

  • We only see the information you choose to share
  • Support emails are handled through our business email
  • We don't share your support requests with third parties
  • We delete support conversations after resolution (unless you request otherwise)

Third-Party Services

TouchBarFix uses these external services:

  • Gumroad: Payment processing (has its own privacy policy)
  • Vercel: Website hosting (has its own privacy policy)
  • Plausible: Privacy-focused analytics (no personal data)

We chose these services because they respect user privacy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to:

  • Request access to
  • Delete or modify
  • Export or transfer

If you have privacy concerns, contact us through the support page.

Children's Privacy

TouchBarFix is safe for users of all ages. We don't collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if we change how TouchBarFix works. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
